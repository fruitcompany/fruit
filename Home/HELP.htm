Graduation Path Analysis Scheduler (GPAS)

 

This is a tutorial only. No log in is required.

Introduction

GPAS lets undergraduates in choosing courses and following the right path to graduation in their majors. It works side-by-side with the interactive Degree Progress Report (DPR).

 

Note: Always use a DPR and GPAS in consultation with your academic advisor.

÷  Step 1

Log in to GPAS at www.gpas.edu with your username and password (Left)

For the first time users, use your CSUN User ID and Password, CSUN Email address and Student ID to create an account (in the right). Be careful while providing this information. You will be logged in automatically after creating a new account.

 

 

÷  Step 2

Your GPAS page displays. Click on the (+) icon to Add Path.

 

 

 

 

÷  Step 3
