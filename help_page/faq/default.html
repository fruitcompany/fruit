Frequently Asked Questions

Need help?  Here's where you can find answers to some of your questions.

Q:How do I add a new schedule path?
Click on the "Plus" to add a new class. You can create multiple paths if you want to.

You will then need to select a major and a starting semester to begin your plan.

 

Q:How do I make adjustments to my existing path?
You can click and drag classes around to fit your desired schedule.  Click a class, hold, and drag the class into another semester.  Then release the class in that semester to place it there.

 

Q: How do I export my schedule or email a counselor for verification?
To export your path, click on the icon that looks like a box with a green arrow going into it. This will export the path in plaintext form. Click on the mail icon to email a counselor about the path on that column.
